Ocean pollution, caused by industrial discharges, plastic waste, and oil spills, poses a global threat. Toxic chemicals harm marine life, leading to species endangerment. Massive plastic debris adversely affects ecosystems, and oil pollution disrupts coastal economies. International collaboration, stringent regulations, and promoting eco-awareness are crucial to mitigate these issues. Only through collective efforts can we safeguard the oceans, ensuring sustainable development.
